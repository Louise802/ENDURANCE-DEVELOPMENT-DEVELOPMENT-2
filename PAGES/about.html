About Us


Who Are We

Endurance Development Enterprise is a 100% BBBEE-compliant organisation founded by Lizzy Ngobeni. We are committed to empowering disadvantaged communities through education, training, and skills development. Our goal is to bridge the gap in computer literacy, IT support, and vocational training, while creating sustainable opportunities for youth, women, and the physically challenged.

Our Vision

To equip, uplift, and improve the quality of education and training – with a focus on computer literacy and technical skills – enabling individuals to succeed in life and contribute to community growth.


Our Mission

We provide affordable, accredited computer education and training that inspires, prepares, and empowers learners to:

  • Gain skills for personal and professional growth
  • Access opportunities in both public and private sectors
  • Continue learning throughout life
  • Thrive in an ever-changing world
